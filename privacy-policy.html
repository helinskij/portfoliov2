Privacy Policy

Overview

This privacy policy explains how jakubhelinski.com ("this website") handles your information. This is a personal portfolio website operated by Jakub Helinski. Your privacy is important and this policy is written to be clear and straightforward.

Data We Collect

This website does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No analytics or tracking scripts are used
  • No advertising networks are present
  • No user accounts or login systems exist
  • No personal data is sent to third parties

Cookies

This website uses only one cookie — a functional cookie stored in your browser's localStorage to remember that you have accepted this cookie notice. This cookie:

  • Contains no personal information
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Is stored locally on your device only
  • Can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser data

No third-party cookies are used on this website.

Contact Form

This website includes a contact form powered by Formspree. If you submit the form, your name, email address, and message are sent directly to Formspree's servers and forwarded to the website owner. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to any data submitted through the form. You can review it at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or Poland, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • The right to access any personal data held about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to withdraw consent at any time

Since this website collects no personal data beyond contact form submissions, there is minimal data to act upon. For any requests related to contact form data, please reach out directly.
